Azure Cloud Servers – Assess, Modernize and Deploy!

Microsoft Azure provides scalable, durable cloud storage, backup and recovery solutions for any data, big and small. It works with the infrastructure you already have and it cost-effectively enhances your business continuity strategy. It also provides the storage required by your cloud applications, including: unstructured text or binary data, such as video, audio and images.

AXEN ELEVATE! solutions

Are designed to make it easier and faster to move the contents of your servers to new hardware in the Cloud and to maximize your existing server(s) as you grow.

Microsoft Azure is also ideal for companies looking to reduce their IT footprint, or who do not want to invest in on-site servers and infrastructure. It provides servers, storage and networking devices hosted in the Microsoft Cloud, with the ability to increase or reduce resources as your needs fluctuate. You retain control over the application and the resources, but the ‘hardware’ is provided and managed by Microsoft, making it tremendously cost-effective.

AXEN ELEVATE! will help you access speed, provide geo-redundancy while your data is replicated to multiple data centers to ensure maximum availability and minimum downtime.

Benefits of the Azure Cloud

  • Reduce costs associated with hosting critical business applications on site
  • Improve application speed, performance and security
  • Streamline and consolidate all information systems into a single secure solution
  • Maintain control and flexibility
